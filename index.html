About Us

JECRC University

JECRC University is located in the culturally vibrant city of Jaipur, Rajasthan, and is known for its strong commitment to academic excellence and innovative approaches. The university's 32-acre campus seamlessly combines classical architectural aesthetics with contemporary landscaping, creating a conducive environment for both learning and research. Situated in the midst of Rajasthan's thriving entrepreneurial ecosystem, our institution is fully devoted to preparing the future leaders, upholding the values of tradition while embracing innovative practices

MakerSpace

Inspired by the genius of Leonardo da Vinci, the Makers Carnival brings together creativity and innovation in a celebration of art, science, and technology. Hosted by JU Makerspace at JECRC University. With project exhibitions, interactive sessions, and hands-on activities, this carnival is a chance for students from schools and colleges to explore and create, just like da Vinci did, pushing the boundaries of imagination and invention. Join us and experience how the past meets the future through technology!

Makers Carnival

The Maker's Carnival, a vibrant two-day event by JUMakerspace at JECRC University! Experience captivating quizzes, inspiring project showcases, and insightful panel discussions on sustainable development goals. Enjoy live music performances and bonfire gatherings as we celebrate creativity with participants from Jaipur's schools and colleges across India. Don't miss this joyful and collaborative experience!

Our Events

This is one of our many exciting events designed to challenge, entertain, and inspire you. Dive in, compete, and enjoy every moment of the experience!

ROBO SUMO

Bots engineered by the participants are brought to the ring for an ultimate showdown where they push their opponent’s bot out of the boundary.

Squid Game

Tech Edition is a three-level tech-infused challenge that puts your creativity, problem-solving, and technical skills to the ultimate test. Inspired by the thrilling games from the series, each level has been reimagined to incorporate innovative technology, creating a unique and immersive experience.

Maze Runner

The Maze Runner Challenge is an exciting event where participants design and program robots to navigate through a complex maze filled with twists, turns, and obstacles. The goal is to reach the finish line in the shortest time while overcoming challenges such as dead ends, sharp corners, and unpredictable paths.

ROBO SOCCER

Bots engineered by the participants put their mini-gladiators on the field and goal towards their win.

ROBO SUMO

Bots engineered by the participants put their mini-gladiators on the field and fight for their win.

Shadow Hunt

Shadow Hunt: Enter the world of darkness, Defeat the shadow lord by solving various challenges in series like dar-shooting, Dancing laser stepping stones, and many more. in an environment of audio-visual spectacles.

Marine Rush

Marine Rush is an exciting event where participants build and operate robotic boats to compete in a series of water-based challenges.This event emphasizes skills in designing efficient boat mechanisms, waterproofing, propulsion systems, and control strategies.

Capture the flag

Bots are put in a one-on-one competition where a bot has to capture the opponent’s flags by using the lasers built in them.
